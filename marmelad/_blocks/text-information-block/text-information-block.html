ABOUT US

At Tekpoint, we are experts in the distribution of telecommunication products and consumer electronics

With offices in Germany, Austria, Benelux, Serbia and Bulgaria, we provide sales services with added value. We strive daily to bring contemporary brands to the doorsteps of European consumers. Because everybody should have a better life through innovation. We achieve this with more than 3.5 million devices delivered annually.

In 2009 we started as a young, innovative start-up with a dynamic team and a big vision: to bring the brands and products of tomorrow into the homes of European consumers. Even today, as a Europe-wide company with more than 120 employees, our goal has remained the same: to improve the daily life of every individual through the latest technology and innovation.
